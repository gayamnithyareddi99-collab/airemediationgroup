The system continuously monitors telemetry patterns to proactively detect unauthorized app usage and misconfigurations. Root cause mapping prevents the same issue recurring by building a permanent institutional memory that survives staff turnover.
Module 03 · EGREE

Evidence-Gated Remediation Execution (EGREE)

The core engine that separates AI confidence from execution authority. System cannot act without verified multi-source evidence eliminating hallucination risk entirely from live IT environments.

EGREE synthesizes telemetry, system logs, and CMDB data before granting execution permission. Low-risk issues like password resets and software patching are resolved autonomously. High-risk actions trigger the AOGM for human analyst approval. False positive rate: under 1%.
Module 04 · AOGM

Analyst Override Governance Memory (AOGM)

When humans override an AI decision, the system captures the governance reasoning not just the fix. This builds a proprietary "Corporate Risk Appetite" dataset unique to each client.

AOGM transforms the service desk role from manual troubleshooting to high-value governance. Analysts "gate" the AI's most powerful capabilities through structured evidence sets (logs, policy links, risk scores). This memory is permanently owned by the client and cannot be replicated by competitors.
Module 05 · PS-RPF

Privilege-Sensitive Resolution Partitioning (PS-RPF)

Creates a secure "DMZ" for high-stakes actions. Automatically identifies tasks involving administrative credentials or security policies and partitions them into a separate workflow requiring Evidence-Clearance.

High-stakes actions modifying firewall rules, granting administrative access, security policy changes are physically and logically separated from general triage agents. This is the technical moat that protects against social engineering attacks that compromise 93% of current breaches.
Module 06 · INAGOE

Governance Output Engine & Audit Shield (INAGOE)

Automatically generates a human-readable "Reasoning Trail" for every action. Ensures EU AI Act Article 12 (Traceability) and Article 13 (Transparency) compliance out-of-the-box.

Every autonomous decision creates a permanent, auditable trail. IT managers can see not just how many tickets were resolved, but the exact reasoning path the AI took. 100% of pilot actions successfully generated multi-source audit logs. Saves compliance teams 10+ hours per quarterly review.

How It Works

From Ticket to Triage.
Logic to Log. Resolution to Reality.

The AI Remediation platform moves beyond static, manual triage. Every incident flows through our Evidence-Gated architecture from detection to autonomous resolution or governed human escalation with a complete audit trail at every step.

The Remediation Pipeline

Six Steps to Zero-Downtime Operations.

01

Ingest & Detect

Real-time system telemetry and incident data ingested via API from ITSM tools (ServiceNow, Jira), CMDB, system logs, and infrastructure monitoring. The IIR engine parses biological-technical freshness constraints.

02

Analyse & Prioritise

Automated root cause mapping links symptoms across silos. Risk-Based Prioritisation ranks incidents by business impact, compliance risk, and infrastructure sensitivity. Access & Identity, Config Drift, Policy Violations identified first.

03

Evidence Gate Check

The DCP-CLM logic gate independently verifies whether AI confidence and policy-based execution permission align. Without multi-source evidence clearance, no action is executed regardless of AI confidence level.

04

Autonomous Remediation

Low-risk, evidence-cleared issues (password resets, software patching, MFA unlocks) are resolved autonomously. Average MTTR reduction: 42%. Each action generates a real-time audit log via INAGOE.

05

Analyst Override Governance

High-stakes actions trigger AOGM presenting the human analyst with logs, policy links, and risk scores. When analysts override, the governance reasoning is captured, building a permanent Corporate Risk Appetite dataset.

06

Audit & Compliance Trail

INAGOE automatically generates human-readable Reasoning Trails for every action (or refused action). 100% EU AI Act Article 12 & 13 compliant. Audit trails saved compliance teams 10+ hours per quarterly review.
